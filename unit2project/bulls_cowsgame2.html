Want to Play a Game?

Bulls and Cows

Rules

The computer will generate a random 4-digit number without any of them repeating.

You will do your best to guess the number in the input field provided. If a digit you entered is not used, you will not get any bulls or cows. If it's in the correct spot, then you'll get 1 bull.

If it's in the wrong spot, it will be a cow. The game ends when you guess the number. The amount of guesses it takes for you to figure it out will be provided.
